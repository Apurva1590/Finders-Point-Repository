Who we are ? What we do ?

Finder’s Point is a website/application which makes it easier to connect to the people and things that matter in your professional world. This is the platform where a person can find list of professionals and information about them and their work to help you hire the professionals to get things done correctly and beneficially; initially it includes services like architect, engineers, plumbers, painters and electricians around you or in your city. It provides you the easiest path to look for the services wherever you want. The value of Finder’s Point is to provide the best services to the customer according to their needs.
